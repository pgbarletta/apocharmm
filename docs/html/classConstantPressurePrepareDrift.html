Create a graph that calculate the net momentums, center of masses(COM), relative coordinates to the COMs, internal momentums, and kinetic energies from the COM motion, and kinetic energies from the internal motions.

All units are SI compatible.

Parameters
absolute_vel_massThe device pointer to the array of absolute velocity and mass of every atom.
absolute_xyzqThe device pointer to the array of absolute atom positions.
relative_xyzqThe device pointer to the array of atom positions relative to the center of mass of their pressure group.
relative_vel_massThe device pointer to the array of relative velocity and mass of every atom.
com_idsThe device pointer to the array of the position of the center of mass of each pressure group, and the atoms that are in the group.
com_momentum_invmassThe device pointer to the array of the group net momentums and inverse masses.
com_kinetic_energyThe xx,yy, and zz components of the groups center of mass kinetic energy.
total_kinetic_energyThe total kinetic energy, used for the thermostat piston.
sorted_atomidsArray of all atom ids sorted by pressure group.
numAtomsnumber of atoms.
numGroupsnumber of groups.
