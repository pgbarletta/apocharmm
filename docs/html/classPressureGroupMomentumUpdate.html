Create a graph that gets the net force on each pressure group and updates the group net momentum.

All units are SI compatible, defining constants (planks constant, speed of light, charge of electron..., and such) may be different.

Parameters
fx,fy,fzthe device pointers to the array of atom forces. These must be in the same order as the atom ids.
old_momentum_invmassThe device pointer to the array of previous net momentums, and the inverse of the net masses, numGroups long.
new_momentum_invmass[out] The device pointer to the array of new net momentums, and the inverse of the net masses, numGroups long.
group_atom_idsThe device pointer to the array of what atoms are in each pressure group.
timestepTime step, how long the force should be applied.
numGroupsNumber of groups.
