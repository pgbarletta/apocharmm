Calculate new box lengths and scaling for center of mass coordinate, momentums and the new kinetic energy.

Only use one thread for this kernel. Uses The Anderson Barostat, with homogenous expansions.

Parameters
old_com_keDevice pointer to kinetic energy of pressure group momentums
old_boxDevice pointer to box lengths
old_box_dotDevice pointer to box length time dirivatives
new_com_keDevice pointer to save post drift pressure group momentum kinetic energy
new_boxDevice pointer to save post drift box lengths
new_box_dotDevice pointer to save post drift box length time dirivatives
com_momentum_scaleDevice pointer to final pressure group momentum scale. This is the total scale from predrift p group momentum to post drift p group momentum.
com_momentum_prescaleDevice pointer to initial pressure group momentum scale. This is the scaleing of momentum that happens before the pressure group centers of mass are moved.
com_position_prescaleDevice pointer to initial pressure group COM scale. This is the scaling p group centers of mass before the are moved by their prescaled momenentums.
ref_pressureReference pressure, units are not atms, it is SI compatible with other units.
timestepTime step.
piston_massPiston Mass, dimensions of mass/length^4.
